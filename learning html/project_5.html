Chicken curry

    Ingredients list

    Main ingridient

  • Chicken – 1.5 lbs (700g), cut into pieces (bone-in for more flavor or boneless)
  • Onions – 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes – 2 medium, chopped or puréed
  • Garlic – 4–5 cloves, minced
  • Ginger – 1-inch piece, grated or minced
  • Green Chilies – 1–2 (optional, adjust to taste)

    • Spices & Seasonings:

  • oil/Ghee – 3 tbsp (vegetable oil, coconut oil, or ghee)
  • Cumin Seeds – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric Powder – 1 tsp
  • Coriander Powder – 1 tbsp
  • Cumin Powder – 1 tsp
  • Red Chili Powder – 1 tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Garam Masala – 1 tsp
  • Salt – To taste
  • Black Pepper – ½ tsp (optional)

    • Liquid & Herbs:

  • Water or Chicken Stock – 1–2 cups (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Fresh Coriander (Cilantro) – ¼ cup, chopped (for garnish)
  • Curry Leaves – 5–6 (optional, for extra flavor)

    • Optional Add-ins:

  • Yogurt or Coconut Milk – ½ cup (for creaminess)
  • Lemon Juice – 1 tbsp (for tanginess)
  • Potatoes or Vegetables (if making a mixed curry)

    Step-by-step instructions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Clean & pat dry 1.5 lbs (700g) chicken (bone-in for flavor or boneless for convenience). Chop 2 onions, 2 tomatoes, 4–5 garlic cloves (minced), 1-inch ginger (grated), and 1–2 green chilies (if using). Measure out all spices for easy access.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Heat 3 tbsp oil/ghee in a deep pan over medium heat. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds and let them sizzle for 10 seconds. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown (~5–6 mins). Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for 1–2 mins until fragrant.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Lower heat and add: 1 tsp turmeric 1 tbsp coriander powder 1 tsp cumin powder 1 tsp red chili powder ½ tsp black pepper Stir for 30 sec to toast spices (don’t let them burn). Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and oil separates (~5–6 mins). Optional: Mash tomatoes for a smoother gravy.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Add chicken pieces, 1 tsp salt, and mix well to coat with masala. Cook on medium-high for 5 mins until chicken turns opaque (but not fully cooked).

  5. step 5:

    6. Pour 1–2 cups water/stock (adjust for desired thickness). Optional: Add ½ cup yogurt or coconut milk for creaminess. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20–25 mins (bone-in) or 15 mins (boneless), stirring occasionally.

  6. step 6:

    7. Sprinkle 1 tsp garam masala and curry leaves (if using). Cook uncovered for 2–3 mins to thicken gravy. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice (optional).

  7. Serve Hot With:

    8. Steamed rice, naan, roti, or bread.
